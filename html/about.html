What We Believe

What do you see when you look at a warm crackling fire? A cozy safe place, inviting atmosphere? Freedom? Freedom from the dependence on others for energy? Freedom from the uncertainty of life? Freedom to choose the way you want to live in your own home?

We believe in empowering the spirit of self reliance by providing a choice of quality appliances that will keep your home warm. Freedom Stoves, LLC provides the very best innovative, cost effective, clean and safe units for you and your family.

As we grow, Freedom Stoves, LLC will continue to improve biomass heating stoves and products. We will strive to expand our role in renewable energy securing a future for generations to come. We invite you to become part of our family and look forward to providing you the freedom to live life on your terms.
